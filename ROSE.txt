ROSE

 A rose is a beautiful flower known for its delicate petals and captivating fragrance. Its blooms come in a variety of colors, including classic red, soft pink, pure white, and vibrant yellow, each often carrying its own symbolism. The petals are velvety and layered, typically forming a tight spiral in the center that gradually opens outward.

They are often associated with love and romance, making them popular choices for gifts and decorations. Whether in a garden, a bouquet, or a single stem, roses evoke feelings of admiration and tenderness.
